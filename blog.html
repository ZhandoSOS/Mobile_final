Taj Mahal: Agra chief symbol of India

The Taj Mahal is a white marble palace, one of the most grandiose monuments of Indian architecture with chic interior decoration and an amazingly beautiful park. The building of the mausoleum creates a unique architectural ensemble in combination with a park, which is divided around it. The entire structure is made of snow-white marble, the semicircular niches penetrate the square facade, and thanks to this it looks as if weightless, despite its massiveness.

Great Wall of Mutianyu, Beijing, China

The Great Wall of China is one of the most ambitious sights in the world. The longest separation wall in the world, an impressive example of ancient defensive architecture. It winds many hundreds of kilometers among impassable terrain and steep mountains. On all sites without exception, tourists enjoy magnificent landscapes. And therefore it is not surprising that the Great Wall of China entered the list of the New Seven Wonders of the World.

CAPPADOCIA: THE WHOLE TRUTH ABOUT ONE OF THE MOST POPULAR INSTA-PLACES OF THE WORLD

Cappadocia is a region in Turkey in Central Anatolia. This is an area with a unique geological structure and natural features, rich in historical and cultural heritage. Cappadocia is one of the most unusual and beautiful places in Turkey with a fabulous lunar landscape, above which at dawn dozens of balloons soar, stunning underground cities, cave churches and houses carved directly in the rocks.

Rice Terraces, China

The most famous rice terraces in China, Longji Rice Terraces, due to their unusual shape, are called the Dragon Ridge. They are located near Guilin County and rise above the sea by 300-1100 meters. This place acquires the most spectacular appearance in spring, when the blue sky and the sun are reflected in the water.
